# BTW Weekly: Week ending 2026-07-13

A third dedicated gas plant for AWS's Madison County data centers entered the permit record this week. Mississippi regulators published a Prevention of Significant Deterioration notice for Entergy's proposed 754 MW combined-cycle facility in Ridgeland, with public comment closing July 14.

## The record

**Traceview Advanced Power Station** — MDEQ issued Air-Construction PSD notice for a 754 MW CCGT to be built by Entergy in Ridgeland, Madison County, Mississippi. The facility would be the third natural gas plant dedicated to AWS data centers in the county. Public comment period ends July 14, 2026.

**Operating capacity revision** — The computed global operating capacity figure was adjusted from 1.1 GW to 1.03 GW on July 13. The cause of the revision has not been documented in the public record.

## New in the funnel

**Jws Blackrock Inc.** — A Texas standard permit application for new registration was received by TCEQ on July 14 for a facility in Pecos County. The applicant name and permit type do not match known data center operators or typical data center air permit categories. Unverified.

**Sentinel-2 overpasses** — Four cloud-affected scenes (30.0–32.3% cloud cover) were captured over xAI Colossus sites 1 and 2 on July 13.

## Watch next

The Traceview PSD comment period closes July 14. MDEQ's processing timeline for the construction permit will indicate whether AWS's reported 2027 energization schedule for additional Madison County capacity remains feasible.
